A bit of
my history

My career in professional football, although now belonging to the past, remains an endless source of pride and unforgettable memories. From my beginnings in the youth divisions, where passion for the ball began to define my life, to the crowded stadiums where my name resonated among chants and applause.

About me

Allow me to share with you some stories and experiences that span from highlighted moments of my past, through the details of my current life, to the aspirations and plans I have reserved for my future.

My past

As a former player, my life revolved around football from childhood, rising from the streets of my city to the grand stadiums, winning titles and representing my country.

My present

Retired from active play, I now focus on imparting my knowledge and experience as a commentator and coach, inspiring future generations.

My future

I aspire to create a football academy that not only develops skills on the field but also promotes values of integrity and leadership.
